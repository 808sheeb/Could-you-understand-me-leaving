# Could you understand me leaving?
a walking simulator
by shihab mian

Game Art
1024x728px
Godot Engine, Blender, VCV Rack

"A home exists as a reflection of those who've lived in it. Spaces share the personalities, dreams and lives of those that have, meticulously over years, adorned its walls and dictated its layout & purpose. How does a home dictate the way we act, how does it affect the perception of our memories? In “Could You Understand Me Leaving?” an empty house screams with a blaring alarm, begging for attention over and over. Asking what can we infer about the life and the motivations of those that occupied this space. Who implemented this alarm? Who was it intended for? What are they trying to tell us? Can we turn it off? How can I ignore it?"
